Welcome

My name is Emily Root.

I'm a software engineer who specializes in creating experiences through games. I'm currently a student at North Carolina State University, graduating in May 2024. Check out all the cool things I've made in projects.

    Gainz: The Financial Literacy Game

    Gainz is an educational game about financial literacy. It was created in for a non-profit educational organization, Katabasis, in 2024. I worked with a team of 5 as the team lead and programmer.

    Check it out

    Cat's Cradle

    Cat's Cradle is a tile-based puzzle game where the player tries to rid its home of pesky dogs. It was created for GMTK Jam 2023 and made in 48 hours. I worked with a team of 6 as a programmer.

    Check it out

    Resource Rush

    Resource Rush is an educational farming game created in Unity in 2023. I converted the project from a javascript-based game to a Unity one and added features and art.

    Winter Break

    Winter Break is a puzzle adventure game where the player is trapped in a snowglobe and must find all the pieces to escape. It was created for a class in 2023. I worked with a team of 4 as a programmer.

    Squidnapped

    Squidnapped is a game created in PuzzleScript about a squid escaping an aquarium by blocking cameras. I created it on my own for a class in 2023.

    Check it out

    Dice Warp

    Dice warp is a tile-based puzzle game built in Unity where the goal is to get the dice to the end with the correct face. I created it on my own for GMTK Jam 2022. I used outside assets for the art.

    Check it out

    God's Year Off

    God's Year Off is a asteroids-style pixel art game. I created it for GMTK Jam 2021 and it was the first game I ever published. I created all of the art and programmed it on my own.

    Check it out
